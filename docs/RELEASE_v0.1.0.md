# Tomb Crossing v0.1.0

Release date: 2026-06-15

## Included Content

- World 1: The Sands of Ra, rooms 1-20.
- World 2: The Royal Crypts, rooms 21-40.
- World 3-5: displayed as `COMING SOON`.
- Three save profiles.
- Room selection and replay for cleared rooms.
- Player statistics, options, and SRAM recovery.
- Original graphics, music, and sound effects.
